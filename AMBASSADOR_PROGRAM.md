## Overview

The Ambassador Program is a structured, merit-based community program designed to grow awareness and adoption of Your Protocol — on-chain FX infrastructure for real-time stablecoin settlement. Ambassadors are not influencers hired for reach. They are people who genuinely understand what This protocol is building and want to help bring it into real networks, real communities, and real use cases.

The program has two distinct pathways: the **Ambassador track**, which is open to all approved applicants and rewards consistent contribution over time, and the **Evangelist track**, which is a selective, time-bound cohort tied to [the program summit Singapore](https://www.token2049.com/). Both tracks use the same application portal and the same scoring infrastructure, but they are evaluated differently and carry different expectations.

## Program Structure

### The Ambassador Track

Anyone who applies through [YOUR_APP_DOMAIN/apply](https://YOUR_APP_DOMAIN/apply) and passes the knowledge test automatically becomes an **L1 Contributor** — no admin action required. Their profile appears on the public leaderboard immediately and XP starts accumulating from their activity.

The YourProtocol team reviews applications and can grant the **L2 Ambassador** designation by clicking "Grant Ambassador" in the admin panel. That is the only thing admin review does — it elevates your rank to Ambassador and awards the L2 badge. Everything else (XP, leaderboard visibility, badges) works the same for all contributors regardless of Ambassador status.

An Ambassador's XP is determined by their activity across 11 components — posting on X, engaging with the community, building tools, participating in Telegram, and more. XP is live: it updates continuously as new data is scraped. A contributor who goes quiet will see their XP decay and their rank fall.

### The Evangelist Track (the program summit)

The Evangelist track is a separate, invitation-only cohort selected from the top-performing Ambassadors ahead of [the program summit Singapore](https://www.token2049.com/). Evangelists are the face of YourProtocol at the event — they represent the protocol in conversations, panels, side events, and social media coverage during the conference.

Evangelists are selected manually by the YourProtocol team. Selection is based on the **Evangelist scoring formula** (see below), which weights content quality and real reach more heavily than the standard Ambassador formula. The number of Evangelists selected per cohort is at the team's discretion, with a typical target of around 12.

The **Evangelist badge** is awarded by admin and can be revoked at any time — including for non-participation, inactivity, or conduct that does not reflect the program's values. It is not a permanent title. The expectation is that Evangelists remain active and visible, particularly on X (Twitter) and in Telegram communities, throughout the lead-up to and during the event.

## Ranks

The public leaderboard displays two earned ranks. These are the only ranks shown publicly, because they are determined purely by score — objective, transparent, and comparable across all Ambassadors.

| Rank | Level | Colour | How It Is Earned |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Contributor** | L1 | Silver (`#C0C0C0`) | Automatic on test pass. Anyone who scores above 0 on the knowledge test is an L1 Contributor from the moment they submit. |
| **Ambassador** | L2 | Green (`#00FF9D`) | Granted by the YourProtocol team. Admin clicks "Grant Ambassador" in the panel. This is the designation your XP and badges orbit around. |

Higher internal designations (Lead Ambassador L3, Ecosystem Lead L4, Full-Time L5) exist within the admin system for internal program management but are not displayed on the public leaderboard. These tiers reflect broader program involvement beyond scoring and are assigned by the team directly.

## The Evangelist Badge

The Evangelist badge is a special designation, distinct from the rank system. It is not earned through points alone — it is awarded by the YourProtocol team to the selected the program summit cohort.

**How it is awarded:** The admin team reviews the top-ranked Ambassadors using the Evangelist scoring formula and selects the cohort manually. The badge is then toggled on in the admin panel for each selected Ambassador.

**Maintenance floor:** Evangelists must maintain their Consistency block (C1 + C2) at 15 or above out of a possible 30 XP, and Total XP at 40 or above, with no active Mission Alignment (C10) escalation. If an Evangelist drops below this floor for 14 consecutive days, the badge steps back automatically. It can be re-awarded once criteria are met again.

**Manual revocation:** The admin can also revoke the badge at any time for non-participation, conduct during the event period, or any reason that does not represent the program appropriately.

**What it looks like:** The Evangelist badge appears on the Ambassador's public profile and on the leaderboard next to their name. It is visually distinct from rank badges — premium, gold-toned, and clearly associated with the program summit.

## XP System

Every contributor has a **Total XP** on a scale of 0–100, composed of 11 components. The leaderboard is ranked by Total XP.

| # | Component | Max XP | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | X Post Output | 12 | Auto — Apify scrape, rolling 14 days |
| C2 | X Posting Spread | 10 | Auto — Apify scrape, rolling 14 days |
| C3 | X Engagement | 14 | Auto — Apify scrape, rolling 14 days |
| C4 | Content Quality | 12 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C5 | Telegram Participation | 8 | Auto — rolling 14 days |
| C6 | Community Value | 10 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C7 | Builder Output | 8 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C8 | Builder Depth | 6 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C9 | Engagement Authenticity | 8 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C10 | Mission Alignment | 7 | Admin-scored, decays 25%/week |
| C11 | Application Quality | 5 | One-time admin score, never decays |
| | **Total** | **100** | |

Auto components update continuously as Apify scrapes run. Admin-scored components decay at 25% per week from the date of last update — a contributor who goes inactive will see their score fall without any admin action needed.

**XP Trend:** the leaderboard shows ↑ RISING, → STABLE, or ↓ FALLING based on week-over-week XP change (threshold: ±2 XP).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I apply if I am not already active on X?**  
Yes, but your score will reflect your activity level. The X-related components (content, engagement, consistency) are scored manually by the admin team based on what they observe. If you are not active on X, those scores will be low.

**How often are scores updated?**  
Scores are updated by the admin team on a rolling basis. There is no fixed schedule — the team reviews activity and updates scores as they observe it.

**Can I lose my Ambassador rank?**  
Your position on the leaderboard is relative — if others improve and your XP stagnates, you drop. The L2 Ambassador designation is granted by the YourProtocol team and can be revoked by the team if needed, but it is not automatically removed based on XP.

**How do I know if I have been selected as an Evangelist?**  
You will be notified by the YourProtocol team directly. The Evangelist badge will appear on your public profile and dashboard.

**What is the difference between the Evangelist badge and the Ambassador rank?**  
The Ambassador rank (L2) is earned through your performance score and is visible to everyone on the leaderboard. The Evangelist badge is a separate, manually awarded designation for the the program summit cohort. You can be an Evangelist without being at Ambassador rank, and you can be at Ambassador rank without being an Evangelist.
